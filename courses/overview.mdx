A course in Frappe Learning is a self-contained unit of educational content. Each course is divided into chapters, and each chapter holds one or more lessons. This three-level hierarchy — Course → Chapter → Lesson — gives instructors a clear way to sequence and group material, while giving learners a predictable structure to navigate.

## Course hierarchy

Every course is made up of three layers:

- **Course** — the top-level record. It holds the title, description, cover image, instructors, pricing, and publishing settings.
- **Chapter** — a grouping of lessons within a course. Chapters carry a title and an ordered list of lessons.
- **Lesson** — the smallest unit of content. A lesson has a title, a markdown body, and optional embedded media such as videos, quizzes, or file uploads.

Lessons in the dashboard are referenced by a two-part index that reflects their position: the chapter number followed by the lesson number within that chapter (for example, `1.1`, `1.2`, `2.1`).

## Enrollment

Learners access course content by enrolling. The enrollment model (`LMS Enrollment`) records the member, the course, and the learner's current progress as a percentage. There are three access patterns:

- **Self-enrollment** — enabled by default. Any logged-in user can enroll directly from the course overview page.
- **Restricted enrollment** — disable self-enrollment on the course settings to require an instructor or moderator to add learners manually.
- **Paid courses** — enable **Paid Course** to gate enrollment behind a payment. The course price, currency, and an optional USD equivalent amount are configured on the course record.

Enrollments can also originate from an **LMS Batch**, in which case the `enrollment_from_batch` field records the source batch.

## Course statistics

The **Dashboard** tab on each course page shows live statistics pulled from the `LMS Enrollment` and `Course Lesson` doctypes:

- **Enrolled** — total number of learners who have enrolled.
- **Average completion rate** — the mean progress percentage across all enrollments.
- **Average rating** — the aggregate of learner reviews.
- **Lessons** — total number of lessons in the course.

The dashboard also shows per-student progress and a lesson completion breakdown sorted either by lesson index or by completion rate.

## Reviews

Enrolled learners can submit a rating and written review through the course overview page. The average rating is stored on the `LMS Course` record and displayed alongside the enrollment count and instructor names at the top of the overview.

## Roles

Two roles have create, write, and delete permissions on the `LMS Course` doctype:

- **Course Creator** — can create and manage their own courses and lessons.
- **Moderator** — has the same permissions as Course Creator plus the ability to publish courses and manage all content on the site.

Instructors assigned to a course must hold the Course Creator or Batch Evaluator role. Only a Moderator can toggle the **Published** switch that makes a course visible to all learners.
